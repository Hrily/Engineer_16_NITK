WORKSHOPS

A technical fest is synonymous to the ‘workshops’ it holds and when the fest happens to be Engineer at NITK, it is bound to be invigorating! Every passing year has seen a commendable growth in the multitude of workshops conducted here, varying from Robotics and Aeromodeling to Forensics and Photography. They focus not only on improving the known knacks but also developing technical dexterity from the scratch.
Attributing to the proficient masters and resources, the workshops attract students from far and near. Engineer ’15 recorded a total of 3000 registrations (excluding spot registrations) for workshops alone!
